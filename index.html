Web Security

How would you advise a potential client to secure his/her website from web security attacks? Note: the word count for your answer should not be greater than 250 words

Buying an SSL security certificate You can get a security certificate for your site for free from many sites such as Cloudflare for example, but this certificate is very important for many different parties and factors such as Google for example and also visitors, it gives great confidence to your site. An SSL certificate creates an encrypted link between your site and the visitor. To put it more simply, an SSL security certificate establishes a secure connection between your site's server and a visitor's web browser.

Use a firewall for site, The firewall is one of the most important and most important means that can protect your website from hacking or malicious visits in general.

Use a global CDN, A content delivery network (or CDN) is a system of connected servers responsible for delivering your website to users or visitors around the world.
